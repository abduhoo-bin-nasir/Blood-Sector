SHADOW BREACH — Asset Drop Guide
=================================

All assets are OPTIONAL. The game runs without them (code draws coloured shapes
for enemies, silence instead of audio). Drop the files below to enable them.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SOUNDS  →  assets/sounds/
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  gunshot.wav       pistol / handgun single shot  
  enemy_hurt.wav    short creature pain grunt  
  player_hurt.wav   short human pain sound  
  door_open.wav     metallic door / transition sound  

Free sources:
  freesound.org — search "pistol shot", "monster hurt", "metal door"
  itch.io       — search "horror sfx free"

MUSIC   →  assets/music/

  ambient.mp3       looping horror/dark atmosphere track  

Free sources:
  freesound.org — search "horror ambient loop"
  itch.io       — search "dark ambient free music"
  incompetech.com (Kevin MacLeod) — filter by "Horror"
